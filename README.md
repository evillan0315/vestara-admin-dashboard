# Why This Repository Exists

Enterprise platforms naturally become more complex as they grow.

New services introduce new dashboards.

Infrastructure introduces new operational tools.

Artificial Intelligence introduces new management workflows.

Security introduces new administrative interfaces.

Analytics introduces another console.

Over time, operations become fragmented across disconnected systems.

The **Vestara Admin Dashboard** was created to eliminate that fragmentation.

Its mission is to provide a single operational workspace capable of managing every service, every organization, every AI capability, and every administrative workflow across the Vestara ecosystem.

This repository represents the operational heart of the platform.

---

# Repository Overview

The dashboard serves as the enterprise command center responsible for monitoring, configuring, and orchestrating platform operations.

Its responsibilities extend beyond traditional administration by integrating AI-native workflows, enterprise governance, operational intelligence, and platform automation into one cohesive experience.

Core operational domains include:

* Enterprise Administration
* Organization Management
* Identity & Access Management
* AI Operations
* Platform Monitoring
* Analytics & Reporting
* Security Operations
* Audit & Compliance
* Integration Management
* Workflow Automation
* System Configuration
* Platform Governance

As the Vestara ecosystem expands, the dashboard will continue to evolve into the primary interface through which administrators, operators, developers, and AI agents collaborate to manage the platform.
