# Vestara Elite Companions — Cinematic T2V Prompt (HappyHorse-1.1-T2V)

This prompt is optimized for the **HappyHorse-1.1-T2V** text-to-video model to
create a premium SaaS marketing video based on the Vestara Elite Companions
dashboard. It is intended for external T2V generation and complements the
in-repo Remotion ad (`src/`). Use it to produce the cinematic "command center"
cut; the Remotion project renders the 30s ecosystem overview locally.

> Model: `HappyHorse-1.1-T2V`
> Brand: Vestara Elite Companions (dark luxury + metallic gold)

## Title

Vestara Elite Companions — Premium Command Center

## Prompt

Create an ultra-premium cinematic SaaS commercial showcasing an AI-powered
luxury management platform called **Vestara Elite Companions**.

The video opens inside a futuristic dark luxury operations center illuminated
with elegant gold lighting. A sleek black-and-gold dashboard materializes from
darkness with glowing metallic reflections. The camera performs a smooth
cinematic dolly-in toward the dashboard as holographic particles float through
the air.

The interface comes alive with fluid premium animations:

- KPI cards animate upward with smooth number counting.
- Revenue graphs draw themselves in glowing gold, blue, and purple lines.
- Circular analytics charts rotate elegantly.
- Upcoming bookings slide in one by one.
- Notifications softly pulse.
- Gold UI highlights shimmer across the interface.
- Icons glow with subtle neon edges.
- Everything feels responsive, premium, and alive.

The camera slowly glides across different sections of the dashboard.

Close-up shots highlight:

- Total Revenue
- Active Companions
- Executive Security services
- Booking Analytics
- Membership Growth
- AI-powered Insights
- Client Satisfaction
- Transaction History
- Live Operations Monitoring

The dashboard is displayed on floating glass panels with metallic gold borders.
Ambient volumetric lighting reflects across the interface while subtle lens
flares enhance the luxurious atmosphere.

Digital particles and elegant golden light streaks sweep across the scene,
emphasizing speed, intelligence, and precision.

The camera performs smooth cinematic pans, parallax movements, orbit shots, and
slow push-ins. Every transition is seamless and premium, similar to Apple's
product launches and high-end enterprise SaaS commercials.

Toward the end, the dashboard zooms outward as the entire interface becomes a
floating holographic command center.

The Vestara "V" logo emerges from the center with polished metallic gold
reflections.

Text fades in: **"Elite Companion Management Platform"** followed by
**"Luxury • Intelligence • Security • Excellence"**.

Final hero shot shows the complete dashboard surrounded by elegant golden
ambient lighting before fading to black.
